How It Works

The Emma Marvel Design Process

A structured, transparent process that keeps you informed and ensures exceptional results every time.

01

Discovery & Brief

We start with a detailed consultation to understand your business, goals, target audience, and vision. I gather all necessary assets and create a clear project brief that guides the entire process.

02

Wireframe & Strategy

Before any visuals are created, I map out the site structure and user flow. Wireframes define the layout and navigation, ensuring every page serves a clear purpose.

03

Design & Prototype

Full visual design is created — brand colors, typography, imagery, and layout all come together. You review and approve the design before a single line of code is written.

04

Development & Testing

The approved design is built into a fully functional, responsive website. Thorough testing across browsers and devices ensures everything works perfectly.

05

Launch & Support

Your website goes live! I handle the deployment and provide 14 days of post-launch support to address any refinements, so you can launch with total confidence.
